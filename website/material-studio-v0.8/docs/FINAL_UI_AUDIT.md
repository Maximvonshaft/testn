# Final UI Audit — Material Studio v0.8

## Decision

**Go for local stakeholder evaluation. No-go for unrestricted public production launch until external business dependencies are supplied.**

## Changes closed

### Brand and differentiation
The previous prototype used a familiar black/gold configurator pattern. v0.8 moves the brand into an architectural-specification register: mineral paper, ink, oxidized copper, blueprint blue and evidence green. The Surface Seam is the only highly distinctive visual device.

### Information architecture
The demo is now a multi-page commercial website rather than one editor screen. Home, Systems, Studio, Projects, Technology & Quality, Resources, About, Contact, Privacy and internal content status have explicit jobs.

### Golden Wall
The material surface follows the true wall quadrilateral: the left top edge begins below the sloped ceiling, the right top reaches the architectural boundary and the base follows the floor junction. Existing light coves remain outside the material plane. A restrained reveal replaces the oversized picture-frame treatment.

### Product truth
Unsupported material/profile combinations are disabled. Public references are not represented as official SKUs. Concept imagery is labelled. Unsupported performance claims remain blocked.

### Conversion
Sample, quote, technical-support and distributor routes validate required fields and consent, generate references, route to an owner/queue/response target, detect duplicates and persist locally.

## Remaining production blockers

1. Manufacturer-approved official product master data and ownership.
2. Verified certification, test, warranty and capacity evidence.
3. Approved installed-project records and media rights.
4. Production CRM/email adapter and delivery monitoring.
5. Permissioned CMS and named content owners.
6. Legal privacy, cookie and retention approval.
7. Production hosting, HTTPS, CDN, backups and observability.
8. Unrestricted Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox, iOS and Android acceptance.
