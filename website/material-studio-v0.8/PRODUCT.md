# Material Studio Product Context

## Product
Material Studio is the commercial website and specification interface for Zhongxin composite surface systems. It presents coordinated wall, wet-area, ceiling, decking, door-wall-cabinet, trim and aroma-layer systems to architects, project owners, contractors, distributors and OEM/ODM buyers.

## Register
Brand-led B2B commercial website with one embedded product experience: the Golden Wall Material Studio.

## Primary audiences
1. Architects and interior designers needing materials, profiles, dimensions, samples and technical files.
2. Hospitality, residential, retail and office project owners needing credible project and quotation support.
3. Contractors and installers needing installation and trim guidance.
4. Distributors and importers needing product-line and commercial support information.
5. OEM/ODM procurement teams needing governed factory evidence and a traceable enquiry route.

## Single job
Help a qualified visitor understand the product system, trust the available evidence, preview one verified wall configuration and take a traceable commercial next step.

## Conversion intents
- Request a sample
- Ask for a project quote
- Request technical support
- Become a distributor

## Content truth boundary
- Do not publish certifications, performance claims, factory capacity, warranty terms or official SKUs without approved evidence.
- Concept imagery is labelled.
- Public configuration references are not represented as official factory SKUs.
- Internal QA language does not appear on customer-facing pages.

## Production boundary
The package is a self-contained commercial integration demo. Public launch still requires approved master data, real CRM/email adapters, named content owners, privacy/legal review, production hosting, monitoring and unrestricted browser/device acceptance.
